# Undaunted Courage

## Overview

The defining characteristic of the Rayatz's leadership — the ability to face down tyrants, buck conventional wisdom, and persevere through unprecedented historical challenges.

## Expression

Throughout his life, the Rebbe demonstrated courage by:

1. **Facing down czarist oppression** in early years
2. **Resisting communist eradication** of Jewish traditions
3. **Adapting Chabad to Western Europe** when forced from Russia
4. **Reimagining Jewish life in America** despite community assimilation
5. **Starting anew repeatedly** in different countries

## Characteristics

- **Bucked conventional wisdom** — Didn't follow expected paths
- **Head-on approach** — Faced obstacles directly
- **Perseverance** — Despite health issues, limited finances
- **Vision** — Held to his vision even when community didn't share it

## The Title

The biography is titled "Undaunted" precisely because this quality defined his approach to every challenge. Through:
- Unparalleled disruption
- Historical transformation
- Physical and spiritual threats to Jewish life

He remained — undaunted.
