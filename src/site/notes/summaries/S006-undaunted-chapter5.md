# Undaunted - Chapter 5: Exile

## Overview

Chapter 5 covers the period of exile in Kostroma following the 1927 arrest — the time between liberation from prison and the final release from exile sentence.

## Key Events

### Release from Prison
- **Date:** July 3, 1927 (3 Tamuz)
- **Context:** After 19 days in captivity
- **Status:** Released but still under exile sentence

### Departure for Kostroma
- **Timing:** July 3, 1927 (same day as release)
- **Mode:** Train journey
- **Sentencing:** 3 years exile in Kostroma
- **Conditions:** Remote location, distance from Jewish community

### Final Liberation
- **Date:** July 12, 1927 (12 Tamuz)
- **Event:** Complete release from exile sentence
- **Significance:** End of 1927 ordeal; return to leadership

## Historical Context

- **GPU tactics:** Release followed by exile was common Soviet technique
- **International pressure:** Continued advocacy contributed to release
- **Health impact:** 19-day imprisonment + travel took physical toll
