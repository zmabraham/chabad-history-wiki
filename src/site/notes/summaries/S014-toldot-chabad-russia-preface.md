# Toldot Chabad bi-Rusya ha-Tzarit - Preface

## Overview

**תולדות חב"ד ברוסיא הצארית** (History of Chabad in Tsarist Russia) is a comprehensive scholarly work documenting the first generation of Chabad-Lubavitch leadership during the Tsarist period. This volume focuses primarily on the Alter Rebbe, Rabbi Shneur Zalman of Liadi (c. 1745-1812), founder of the Chabad movement.

## Key Topics Covered

### Halachic Works
- **שלחן ערוך** (Shulchan Aruch) - Orach Chaim - The Alter Rebbe's code of Jewish law
- **קונטרסי הלכות** (Kuntreisim) - Halachic booklets on various topics
- **שו"ת צמח צדק** (Responsa Tzemach Tzedek) - Rabbinic correspondence
- **הלכות נטילת ידים** (Laws of Handwashing) - Example of halachic methodology

### Historical Period
- **Years covered:** Approximately 1777-1797 (תקל"ז - תקנ"ז)
- **Locations:** Mohilev, Vitebsk, Liadi, Lubavitch
- **Key events:** The Alter Rebbe's travels, settlement in different communities

### The Document Structure
The work is organized into **656 chapters** covering:
- The Alter Rebbe's halachic writings and publication history
- His activities and influence in Russia
- Interactions with authorities (including arrests in 1798 and 1801)
- Establishment of educational institutions
- Community leadership and correspondence

## Key Figures

| Person | Role | Period |
|--------|------|--------|
| \[\[Alter Rebbe\]\] | Founder of Chabad | 1745-1812 |
| Rabbi Menachem Mendel of Vitebsk | Leader of aliyah | Contemporary |
| The Mitteler Rebbe | Successor | 1773-1827 |
| The Tzemach Tzedek | Grandson, author of responsa | 1789-1866 |

## Geographic Locations

- **מאהליב (Mohilev)** - Where Alter Rebbe stayed 1777-1778
- **ויטבסק (Vitebsk)** - Major Chabad center
- **לאדי (Liadi)** - Alter Rebbe's final settlement
- **לובאוויטש (Lubavitch)** - Later became Chabad capital
