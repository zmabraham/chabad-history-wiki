# Rabbi Shalom Dovber Schneersohn (The Rashab)

## Overview

Fifth Lubavitcher Rebbe (1860-1920), father of the Rayatz. Instilled in his son the sense of mission that would define his leadership.

## Key Facts

- **Born:** 1860
- **Died:** 1920
- **Title:** The Rashab (acrostic of his name)
- **Children:** Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak (The Rayatz)
- **Brother:** Rabbi Zalman Aharon (The Raza)

## Significance

- Father and mentor to the sixth Rebbe
- Instilled the sense of mission in the Rayatz
- Fifth in the line of Lubavitcher Rebbes
