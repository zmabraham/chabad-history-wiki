# Rabbi Elchanan Dovber Morosow

Secretary and close chassid of the Frierdiker Rebbe, Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak Schneersohn.

## Role

- Served as the Rebbe's **personal secretary**
- Responsible for managing the Rebbe's correspondence and schedule
- Present during key events including the 1927 crisis

## The Warning (1924)

After consultations in Moscow and the Rebbe's election as leader of the organization, Reb Elchanan Dovber met the Rebbe on the journey back to Rostov with an urgent warning:

> *"agents of the local G.P.U. were waiting to imprison him and send him into exile for his work to strengthen religion."*

This warning prompted the Rebbe to:
- Turn around on the road
- Stay in a small village near Moscow until the fury subsided
- Return to Rostov on the 11th of Nissan
- Eventually agree to leave Rostov voluntarily for Leningrad
