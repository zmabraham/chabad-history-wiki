# Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson (The Ramash / Seventh Rebbe)

## Overview

Seventh Lubavitcher Rebbe (1902-1994), son-in-law and successor of the Rayatz. Known as the Ramash until 1950, then referred to as the seventh Rebbe.

## Key Facts

- **Born:** 1902
- **Died:** 1994
- **Title:** The Ramash (until 1950), then The Seventh Rebbe
- **Father-in-law:** Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak Schneersohn (The Rayatz)
- **Married:** Daughter of the Rayatz

## Significance

- Second son-in-law of the Rayatz
- Successor to the sixth Rebbe
- Led the global expansion of Chabad
