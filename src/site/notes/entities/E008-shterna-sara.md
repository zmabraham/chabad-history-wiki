# Rebbetzin Shterna Sara Schneersohn

## Overview

Mother of the Rayatz (sixth Lubavitcher Rebbe) and wife of the Rashab (fifth Rebbe). Known for her prophetic dream that foretold her son's birth.

## Key Facts

- **Marriage:** 1875 to Rabbi Shalom Dovber (Rashab)
- **Origin:** Ovruch, Ukraine
- **Marriage journey:** Moved 400 miles from Ovruch to Lubavitch
- **Children:** Yosef Yitzchak (the Rayatz)

## The Prophetic Dream (1879)

On Simchat Torah 1879, Rebbetzin Shterna Sara experienced a significant event:

### The Blessing Incident
- Her father-in-law, Rabbi Shmuel (Maharash), blessed each family member
- He overlooked her, didn't give her a blessing
- She was left in tears

### The Dream
That night, she dreamed of three Jews who promised her a son. The three were identified as:
1. The Alter Rebbe (Rabbi Schneur Zalman)
2. The Mitteler Rebbe (Rabbi Dovber)
3. The Tzemach Tzedek (Rabbi Menachem Mendel)

### Fulfillment
- Yosef Yitzchak was born the following year (June 30, 1880)
- The dream was recognized as prophetic

## Family Life

### Yalta Period
The family spent time in Yalta, allowing young Yosef Yitzchak quality time with his parents. During these walks:
- Father studied scholarly books
- Rabbi Schneur Zalman taught Yosef Yitzchak for an hour daily
- Mother read letters, sewed, and knit

### Supportive Role
When her husband experienced headaches (during leadership transition period), Yosef Yitzchak discovered her in tears and conveyed messages of reassurance between them.

## Significance

- Mother of the sixth Rebbe
- Her prophetic dream confirmed divine providence in her son's birth
- Her marriage to her cousin (Rashab) continued the family line
