# Rabbi Zalman Aharon (The Raza)

## Overview

Brother of the Rashab (fifth Rebbe) and uncle of the Rayatz (sixth Rebbe).

## Key Facts

- **Title:** The Raza (acrostic)
- **Relationship:** Brother of Rabbi Shalom Dovber Schneersohn (The Rashab)
- **Nephew:** Rabbi Yosef Yitzchak Schneersohn (The Rayatz)
