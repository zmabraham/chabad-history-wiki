# R. Nissan

The teacher of the young Yosef Yitzchak (later the Frierdiker Rebbe) who advised him to begin recording his recollections in a book.

---

## What We Know

- Taught the young Rayatz in Lubavitch
- In **5652 (1892)**, advised Yosef Yitzchak (then age 12) to begin recording his recollections
- The Rayatz followed this directive and began a memoir book
- The **first imprisonment** (1892, age 11) was recorded in this book in 5653 (1893)

---

## Significance

R. Nissan's guidance led to the creation of what would become the foundation for *Likkutei Dibburim* — the multi-volume work containing the Rebbe's memoirs and historical accounts.

The young Rayatz wrote of this teacher's directive:

> "At that time, following the advice and directive of my teacher, R. Nissan, I began (in 5652 [1892]) to record my recollections in a book."
