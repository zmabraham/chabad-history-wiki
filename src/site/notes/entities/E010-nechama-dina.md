# Rebbetzin Nechama Dina Schneersohn

## Overview

Wife of the Rayatz (sixth Lubavitcher Rebbe) and his second cousin. Their marriage led directly to the founding of Yeshivas Tomchei Temilim.

## Key Facts

- **Relationship:** Second cousin of Rayatz
- **Father:** Rabbi Avraham Schneersohn of Kishinev
- **Engagement:** June 9, 1896 (28 Sivan, 5646)
- **Wedding:** September 10, 1897 (13 Elul, 5657)
- **Location:** Lubavitch

## Engagement and Wedding

### Engagement (1896)
- **Location:** Bolivke (summer resort where family was staying)
- **Duration:** Week-long celebration
- **Tena'im signed:** June 9, 1896

### Wedding (1897)
- **Date:** September 10, 1897 (13 Elul)
- **Timing:** Two weeks before Rosh HaShanah
- **Location:** Courtyard of main synagogue in Lubavitch
- **Attendees:** Chasidim streamed to Lubavitch
- **Details:** Rayatz donned shtreimel for first time
- **Celebration:** Continued Sunday evening in the zal

## Significance

The marriage connection was chosen over wealthier prospects, prioritizing family connection over financial benefit.

## Founding of Tomchei Temilim

The wedding directly led to the founding of Yeshivas Tomchei Temilim:
- Rayatz asked his father to open a yeshivah as his wedding gift
- On wedding reception Sunday (September 12), Rashab announced the plan
- This revolutionary institution would integrate Talmud and chasidic philosophy
