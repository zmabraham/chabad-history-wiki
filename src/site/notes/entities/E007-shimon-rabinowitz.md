# Shimon Rabinowitz

## Overview

Childhood friend of young Yosef Yitzchak Schneersohn in Lubavitch. Present during the first arrest incident at age 11.

## Key Event

### The Marketplace Walk (1891)
- **Date:** June 29, 1891
- **Location:** Shielve Street, Lubavitch
- **Activity:** Walking from school to eat lunch with Yosef Yitzchak
- **What happened:** They met Reb Dovid the butcher, then encountered the police incident
- **Yosef Yitzchak's account:** "The market was packed with farmers, horses, and buggies"

## Significance

Witness to the first demonstration of Yosef Yitzchak's fearless defense of Jews.
