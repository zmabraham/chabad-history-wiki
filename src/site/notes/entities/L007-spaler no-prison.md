# Spalerno Prison

The prison in Leningrad where the Frierdiker Rebbe was incarcerated during his 1927 imprisonment.

---

## 1927 Imprisonment

The Rebbe Rayatz wrote a maamar of Chassidus on **Rosh Chodesh Tammuz, 5687 (1927)** during his incarceration at Spalerno Prison. This maamar was published as a separate booklet.

### Conditions at Spalerno

From the Rebbe's account:
- Midnight arrest: **2:15 AM on 15 Sivan 5687**
- Duration: **18 days, 11 hours, 15 minutes** of imprisonment
- Interrogation and intimidation by GPU officers
- Humiliation of prison routine
- The Rebbe described these as days of "affliction of the body and suffering of the spirit"

---

## Spiritual Activity During Imprisonment

Despite the conditions, the Rebbe engaged in spiritual work:
- Wrote the maamar on Rosh Chodesh Tammuz
- Maintained his composure and faith ("I have not feared them, I do not fear them, and I shall not fear them")
- Viewed the suffering as "luminous days" that elevated his spiritual level
